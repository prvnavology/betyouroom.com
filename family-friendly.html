Family Friendly

Our Friendly Family Hotels Page is designed to help you find the perfect accommodations that cater to the needs and desires of every family member, ensuring a memorable and enjoyable stay for all.

These hotels provide the ideal setting for creating cherished memories, with activities and services tailored to engage and delight guests of all ages.

Imagine arriving at a hotel where children are greeted with excitement and a variety of activities designed just for them. Indoor and outdoor play areas, dedicated kids' clubs, and engaging programs ensure that younger guests are entertained and cared for, giving parents the opportunity to relax and unwind. Family-friendly dining options cater to all tastes, offering delicious and nutritious meals that everyone can enjoy.

Aqua Palace Hotel, Prague

Aqua Palace Hotel is a top choice for families, featuring one of the largest water parks in Central Europe. Highlights include:

Water Park Access:Direct access to the on-site Aquapalace Water Park with slides, wave pools, and a lazy river.

Family Rooms: Spacious family suites and interconnected rooms.

Check Hotel

Orea Resort Horal, Špindlerův Mlýn

Nestled in the Krkonoše Mountains, Orea Resort Horal offers a blend of adventure and relaxation for families. Features include:

Outdoor Activities:Skiing in winter, hiking, and biking trails in summer.

Family Accommodations:Rooms and suites designed for families, with additional beds and cribs available.

Kids' Play Area: Indoor and outdoor play areas, plus a children's pool.

Check Hotel

Hotel Horizont, Pec pod Sněžkou

Situated in the Giant Mountains, Hotel Horizont is perfect for active families. It offers:

Adventure Programs: Guided mountain tours, skiing, and snowboarding in winter, mountain biking in summer.

Check Hotel

Lipno Lake Resort, Lipno nad Vltavou

Located on the banks of Lake Lipno, this resort is ideal for water-loving families. Features include:

Water Sports: Kayaking, sailing, and windsurfing on Lake Lipno.

Adventure Park: A treetop adventure park and toboggan runs.

Check Hotel

Hotel Duo, Prague

Hotel Duo combines city exploration with family-friendly amenities. Highlights include:

Swimming Pool: Indoor pool for relaxation and play.

Proximity to Attractions: Easy access to Prague's top family-friendly attractions, such as Prague Zoo and Botanical Garden.

Check Hotel

Wellness Hotel Frymburk, Frymburk

This lakeside hotel offers a variety of activities and amenities for families. Features include:

Water Park: On-site aqua park with slides, pools, and water attractions.

Outdoor Activities: : Biking, hiking, and fishing around Lake Lipno.
